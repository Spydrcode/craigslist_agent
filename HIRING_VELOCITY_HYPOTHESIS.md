# The Hiring Velocity Hypothesis

## Core Insight: Active Hiring = Desperate for Help

### **The Hypothesis**

Companies posting **multiple active job listings** are the BEST prospects because:

1. **They're Growing Rapidly**
   - Can't grow fast enough with current team
   - Need immediate help

2. **Budget is Approved**
   - Headcount = money already allocated
   - They WILL spend to solve the problem

3. **They're Desperate**
   - Posting multiple jobs = can't find enough people
   - Willing to pay for external help

4. **They Have Urgency**
   - Can't wait months to hire and onboard
   - Need solutions NOW

## Disqualifiers (Auto-Skip)

Even if they have many jobs, skip if:

1. **Staffing/Recruiting agency**
   - They're not the end client
   - Just posting jobs for others

2. **Non-tech company**
   - Restaurant chain hiring "web developer"
   - Low tech sophistication

3. **Spam indicators**
   - "Make money from home"
   - "No experience needed"
   - MLM keywords
